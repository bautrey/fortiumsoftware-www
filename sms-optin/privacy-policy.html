SMS Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2026 | Last Updated: April 14, 2026

This SMS Privacy Policy supplements the Fortium Partners Privacy Policy and describes how Fortium Partners, LLC ("Fortium," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our SMS messaging program.

1. Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive SMS notifications from Fortium Partners, we collect:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • Your opt-in consent status and date
  • Opt-out requests and dates
  • Message delivery status

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your mobile phone number solely to send you the SMS notifications you have opted in to receive, including:

  • Invoice and payment confirmations
  • Task and billing request reminders
  • Onboarding and account notifications
  • Critical system alerts

3. No Sharing of Mobile Information

No mobile information, including your phone number and SMS consent data, will be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We will never sell, rent, loan, trade, lease, or otherwise transfer your mobile phone number or any information collected through our SMS program to any third party or lead generator.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your mobile information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your mobile phone number is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel who require it to operate the SMS notification service.

5. Opting Out

You may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message you receive from us
  • Updating your Communication Preferences in PartnerConnect
  • Contacting us at privacy@fortiumpartners.com

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. You will not receive any further SMS messages unless you opt back in by replying START or re-enabling SMS in your account settings.

6. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your account activity and notification preferences. You control which notification types you receive through your PartnerConnect Communication Preferences.

7. Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Fortium Partners does not charge for SMS notifications, but your mobile carrier's standard messaging rates apply.

8. Help

For assistance with our SMS program, reply HELP to any message or contact us at privacy@fortiumpartners.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this SMS Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued participation in the SMS program after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
